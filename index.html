Bio

I was born in Baku, Azerbaijan. I grew up in Ankara, Türkiye. I received my Ph.D. in Operations Research from Stanford University in 2022, under the supervision of Itai Ashlagi and Itai Gurvich. I received my B.S. in Mathematics from Bilkent University in 2016. I am interested in market design, matching markets, online platforms, and applied probability.
